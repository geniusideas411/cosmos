COSMOS Study Center,Deoghar

COSMOS Study Center was established on August 2015 which is situated in the pious city of Baidyanathdham Deoghar at Castairs Town,Sundar Lal Mishra Road. The institute was started with the purpose of value based education with character building to youngsters and not only for bookish learning. Institute's motto is knowledge is that which liberates, which is indeed the ultimate goal of every human being.

From The Director's Desk

Education is the process of facilitating learning, or the acquisition of knowledge, skills, values, beliefs, and habits. Educational methods include teaching, training, story telling, discussion and directed research. Education frequently takes place under the guidance of educators, however learners can also educate themselves. Education can take place in formal or informal settings and any experience that has a formative effect on the way one thinks, feels, or acts may be considered educational.

-Msr. Shuvam Raha
